# CyberSecurity
- cyber security is the practice of protecting computer,networks,data from attacks and unauthorized accesss.
- 
**1.CIA Triad**

- Confidentiality:
    Data should only be accessed to authorized ppl.
    Example:Password.
- Integrity:
    Data must remain Accutarte and unchanged unless modifies y the user.

- Availability:
- Systems and data must be available when needed by authorized person.
    Ex: Backup systems.
    
**2. Types of cyber Threats**

- Phishing:- Fake Emails to steal info
- Malware: It is Combo ofmalicious software which desinged to damage or access your system.
- Ransomware:
   Malware that locks or encrypts your files and demand money to unlock them.
- Dos attack:
   attackers flood a website or server with heavy traffic.
- Insider Threat:
  Threat from emoloyee,Contractors or internal people.
  Ex: An employee who resigned copies customer data to a pen drive and sells it to competitors.
- Man in the Middle attack:
  Attacker secretly intercepts communication btw two ppl.
